Fajitas

Tired of that take out food and want something thats quick, easy to make and healthy? Well Fajitas are the way to go. Originally a classic Mexican recipe, fajitas consist of grilled meat (usually chicken, beef, or shrimp) that is served along with vegetables and tortillas. What also makes them popular is that they are high in protein, fiber, low in cholesterol You cant beat this healthy family recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken, Beef, Shrimp, etc (No meat for vegetarians)
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Fajita Seasoning. Add any other spices of your liking such as (chili, cumin, garlic, paprika, oregano, and pepper)
  • Lime juice
  • Vegetables of choice (Corn, beans, carrots, etc)
  • Tortillas

Procedure

  1. Season the meat. Sprinkle the seasoning on all sides of the meat and dont be afraid to use your fingers to press it within the meat
  2. Heat a large skillet pan and lightly coat it with a drizzle of oil of your choice (olive, avocado, canola, vegetable are examples)
  3. Sear and cook the meat for about 7-10 minutes one each side until it is cooked thoroughly
  4. While the meat is cooking begin preparing/cutting your additional toppings
  5. Remove the meat from the pan and set them aside for a few minutes before you begin slicing into smaller strips
  6. Add your prepared toppings into the pan until caramelized
  7. Add the cut meat back into the pan and squeeze fresh lime juice on top and stir everything together
  8. Stir for a few minutes and serve immediately with tortillas and extra toppings
